    Structure that works

    1. Hook (1 short paragraph): drop the reader into a specific moment.
    2. Story (2–3 paragraphs): what happened, what you did, what it felt like.
    3. Turn: what changed in how you think or act.
    4. So what: connect it to who you are now and who you're becoming.
