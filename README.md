# ◆ PATHFINDER — College Guidance System

A free, private college-planning app for high school students, with an igloo.inc-inspired visual system: near-black ice theme, wireframe 3D crystal intro, film grain, monospace HUD typography, custom cursor, and staggered reveal animations. Everything runs in your browser — no account, no server, and your data never leaves your device (saved in localStorage).

**Features**

- **Cinematic intro** — preloader, animated wireframe ice crystal, particle field
- **Dashboard** — deadline countdowns, grade-based roadmap, task queue, progress ring
- **Profile** — school, GPA, SAT/ACT, clubs & activities, leadership, honors & awards, weighted-GPA converter, pinned programs & scholarships
- **Major Match** — pick your major directly or take an 8-question interest quiz
- **Course Planner** — 4-year high school class plans for 8 different major tracks
- **Colleges** — 34-school database **plus add-your-own schools**, personalized ~% admission-chance estimates, Reach/Match/Safety badges, list-balance meter, and a per-college application requirements tracker
- **Essay Coach** — Common App prompts, brainstorming, autosaving draft editor with instant feedback (word count, cliché detection, style checks)
- **Opportunities** — searchable summer programs and scholarships, saveable to your profile

**Run it:** just open `index.html` in any browser — no build step, no dependencies.

> ⚠️ College stats, deadlines, and admission-chance estimates are approximate planning aids, not guarantees. Always verify details on official college and program websites.
