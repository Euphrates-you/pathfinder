# 🧭 Pathfinder — Your College Counselor

A free, private college-planning app for high school students. Everything runs in your browser — no account, no server, and your data never leaves your device (saved in localStorage).

**Features**

- 🏠 **Dashboard** — deadline countdowns, grade-based roadmap, to-do list, progress ring
- 👤 **Profile** — school, GPA, SAT/ACT, clubs & activities, leadership, weighted-GPA converter
- 🎯 **Major Match** — pick your major directly or take an 8-question interest quiz
- 📚 **Course Planner** — 4-year high school class plans for 8 different major tracks
- 🏛️ **Colleges** — 34-school database with personalized ~% admission-chance estimates, Reach/Match/Safety badges, list-balance meter, and a per-college application requirements tracker
- ✍️ **Essay Coach** — Common App prompts, brainstorming, autosaving draft editor with instant feedback (word count, cliché detection, style checks)
- 🌟 **Opportunities** — searchable summer programs and scholarships
- 🌙 Dark mode

**Run it:** just open `index.html` in any browser — no build step, no dependencies.

> ⚠️ College stats, deadlines, and admission-chance estimates are approximate planning aids, not guarantees. Always verify details on official college and program websites.
